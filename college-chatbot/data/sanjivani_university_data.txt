=== SANJIVANI UNIVERSITY OVERVIEW ===
Sanjivani University is a private university located in Kopargaon, Ahmednagar district, Maharashtra, India. It was established on the 40th anniversary of the Sanjivani Rural Education Society. === HOSTEL AND MESS FACILITIES ===
Sanjivani University provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls.
Hostels include basic amenities such as furnished rooms, internet access, security, and hygiene services.
Mess facilities are available within the campus providing regular meals for hostel students.
